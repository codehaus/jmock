Logo Competition

Don't like the jMock logo? Neither do we! Think you can do better? Prove it!

We need a new logo for the jMock site and other publicity material but none of us are graphic designers. Therefore we're holding a competition to choose the design of the new logo. To enter, send us logos for both jMock and nMock sites in a SVG, Postscript or PDF format and we will choose our favourite. Entries must arrive in our mailing boxes by February 28th, 2004. The winner must be willing to transfer copyright for the logos to the jMock and nMock teams.
